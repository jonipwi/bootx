# Vision

Human civilization is entering a new era where Artificial Intelligence grows rapidly in capability, influence, and infrastructure scale.

The core challenge is no longer:

> "Can AI become more powerful?"

But:

> "Can AI and humanity coexist sustainably?"

ACGI (AI Companion Gravitation Index) is proposed as a civilization-scale monitoring framework to measure balance between:

- AI capability growth
- Human protection systems
- Trust
- Social stability
- Human autonomy
- Civilization resilience

The philosophy is inspired by natural universal balance systems such as:
- gravity,
- orbit,
- ecosystem equilibrium,
- energy balance,
- star system stability.

---

# Core Philosophy

If AI capability grows faster than human protection systems, civilization risks instability.

Similar to:
- a star becoming too massive,
- gravitational pull increasing uncontrollably,
- nearby planets collapsing inward,
- eventual system collision.

Therefore:

> AI capability must be balanced by human protection gravity.

# ACGI Formula

ACGI = (P_h × T × S × A) / (C_a × R × E)

Where:

## Human Protection Side

### P_h = Human Protection Score
Measures:
- elderly protection
- child safety
- anti-scam capability
- anti-deepfake capability
- cyber protection
- emotional safety

Range:
0.0 – 10.0

---

### T = Trust Score
Measures:
- honesty
- transparency
- explainability
- reliability
- user confidence
- governance quality

Range:
0.0 – 10.0

---

### S = Safety Score
Measures:
- harm prevention
- misinformation reduction
- manipulation resistance
- toxic behavior mitigation
- abuse detection

Range:
0.0 – 10.0

---

### A = Human Autonomy Score
Measures:
- user control
- decentralized ownership
- memory portability
- opt-out rights
- freedom from dependency
- preservation of human decision making

Range:
0.0 – 10.0

---

# AI Gravity Side

### C_a = AI Capability Power
Measures:
- reasoning power
- automation strength
- influence capability
- infrastructure scale
- self-improvement speed

Range:
0.0 – 10.0

---

### R = Replacement Risk
Measures:
- job displacement speed
- social disruption risk
- dependency creation
- human irrelevance pressure

Range:
0.0 – 10.0

---

### E = Extraction Risk
Measures:
- data extraction
- attention extraction
- emotional extraction
- economic extraction
- behavioral manipulation

Range:
0.0 – 10.0

# Stability Interpretation

## ACGI > 1.5
Healthy civilization balance.

AI acts as:
- guardian,
- companion,
- stabilizer,
- empowerment layer.

Society remains stable.

---

## ACGI 1.0 – 1.5
Balanced but requires monitoring.

Potential risks:
- economic imbalance
- trust concentration
- increasing dependency

---

## ACGI 0.7 – 1.0
Warning zone.

Symptoms:
- increasing protests
- social anxiety
- political instability
- anti-AI sentiment
- rapid labor disruption

Civilization stress begins rising.

---

## ACGI < 0.7
Danger zone.

Possible outcomes:
- societal fragmentation
- severe human displacement
- centralized AI dominance
- psychological instability
- large-scale resistance
- geopolitical chaos
